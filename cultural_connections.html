Cultural Connections



Novacare is committed to ensuring that all eligible individuals have the greatest opportunity to make use of and participate in the full range of activities and services available.

Please tell us if you, or the person you care for, needs help to make use of our services. Assistance that can be provided includes:

  • Information translated into other languages
  • Information in large format print or audio recording
  • Services that are delivered in a culturally appropriate manner
  • Access to interpreter services
  • Links to ethnic community groups and activities
  • Help with navigating the health and aged care sectors through liaison and coordination with other health providers

Novacare values cultural diversity and we welcome your suggestions and feedback on how our organisation can support people from culturally diverse backgrounds.
