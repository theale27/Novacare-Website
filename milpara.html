Milpara: Gathering of the Community



Formally known as Seniors Club, Milpara (meaning "Gathering of the Community") is the place to be for social stimulation with centre based activities, lots of great outings and physical exercise classes.

Transportation can be arranged to/from Milpara.

Novacare Milpara:
Location: 26-30 Caldwell Street, Merewether
Ph: 4963 6356 or 1300 363 654

Novacare Milpara has loads of great outings and activities planned for April, including:

  • Fort Scratchley Guided Tour and Canon Firing
  • Trip out to Budgewoi
  • Grossman House tour and lunch
  • Concert at Milpara with Adam Price
  • Trip out to Oakvale Farm
  • Performance at Milpara by Ken Cocking
  • Trip to Inner Light House at Nelson Bay
