Feedback



Click here for a pdf guide and form on providing feedback on Novacare services.

Novacare is committed to the following principles:

  • The right of people to make choices about their lives
  • The right of people to dignity, respect, privacy and confidentiality
  • The right of people to be valued as individuals
  • The right of people to access services without discrimination
  • Accountable and responsive services

We welcome your feedback and endeavour to use this information to constantly improve our services.

Compliments are a great way to recognise and thank a care worker who has done a great job. All compliments are passed on to the care worker and their supervisor and, when appropriate, we provide a small token of recognition.

Complaints are treated in the strictest confidence and Novacare would like to hear from you if you have any comments or concerns about the service you receive. You are welcome to have an advocate of your choice act on your behalf to communicate with us.

Contact Novacare either by telephone or in writing, to inform us of the issue and to receive a copy of our Client Grievance Procedure.
