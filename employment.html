Care Workers If you are highly motivated, enthusiastic and interested in joining the Novacare team we would like to hear from you. You must have a demonstrated commitment to caring, relevant experience, be willing to undertake additional in-house training, and to undergo criminal record and working with children checks.

Community Care Workers Domestic Assistance
Care workers in our Domestic Assistance Program provide housekeeping, shopping and bill paying assistance services.

Community Care Workers Certificate III
Care workers in our Community Aged Care (CACPs), Extended Aged Care at Home (EACH) and Extended Aged Care at Home Dementia (EACHD) Packages work in private homes in the Newcastle area and at the Ailsa Craig Respite Cottage in Hamilton.

Volunteers
Novacare welcomes the assistance of volunteers. Novacare provides a safe and healthy working environment.
