Dementia Services



Novacare is renowned for our specialist services for people living with dementia.
Services focus on the whole person and include in-home packaged care, centre based day care, activities, outings and respite services for carers. Our Client Services Manager can visit you at home to explain the options available to you and to help find solutions that will enhance your quality of life and that of the person you care for.

EACH dementia packaged care
A range of flexible support activities can be tailored into a package to help people with dementia to remain at home for as long as possible and to support carers. We deliver services as part of the Extended Aged Care at Home â€“ Dementia (EACH Dementia) program funded by the Australian Government.

Our experienced dementia care staff can assist with:

  • Personal care
  • Dressing or undressing
  • Mobility and transfers
  • Meal perperation and assistance with nutitional needs
  • Medication supervision
  • Domestic assistance including housekeeping and laundry
  • Respite
  • Additional support during periods of specific need


Dementia specific centre based day care
Our dementia-specific centre based day care program includes outings, day trips, entertainment and centre based activities that are thoroughly planned and adjusted to meet the changing needs of each care recipient. Groups are kept small; enabling one to one interaction in a calm atmosphere. Respite is available as a one-off or on a regular basis. The service is available from 8am to 4pm, Monday to Friday and is funded by the Australian Government and NSW Government Human Services - Ageing, Disability and Home Care.

Overnight, emergency and short term respite
The Ailsa Craig Respite House at Hamilton is available to carers who require short term or emergency overnight respite. The centre provides a modern, home-like environment and can accommodate up to six people for overnight stays. Carers can take a short break with the comfort of knowing that their loved one is being cared for by qualified care staff in a comfortable, secure setting. The service is available seven days and evenings per week.

Carer Information
Our experienced staff remain up to date with the latest information on dementia care. They are available for a confidential chat or support and advice. Carers can join a carers group and access a variety of informative resources at Ailsa Craig Respite Cottage.

We also deliver packaged care and respite services to people who are not eligible for government-funded programs.
Please contact us to discuss your needs.
