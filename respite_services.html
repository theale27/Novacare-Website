Respite Services



Novacare provides respite care to suit people in most situations.
Respite care benefits families and loved ones alike. Our highly trained staff are sensitive to the needs of carers and understand that it can be difficult to leave your loved ones. Staff assist families to decide on appropriate respite care and what actions can be taken to ensure the experience is positive for all involved.

Overnight, emergency and short-term respite
The Ailsa Craig Respite Cottage at Hamilton is available to carers who require short term or emergency overnight respite. Funded respite places are available to most carers. Our staff provide advice on a range of funding options and how you can access them. The cottage provides a modern, home-like environment and can accommodate up to six people for overnight stays. Ailsa Craig Respite Cottage is open 24 hours / 7 days for 48 weeks of each year. More information

Centre based adult day care and respite
Novacare Seniors Club provides centre based day care and social support to people living alone or with a carer. The lively activity plan includes outings and entertainment. An individual care plan is developed catering to the care needs and special interests of each client. Personal care such as assistance with toileting is included. Transport and meals are provided. The centre is open from Monday to Friday between the hours of 8:00am - 4:00pm.

Dementia specific day care respite
Our dementia-specific centre based day care program includes outings and day trips, entertainment and centre based activities that are thoroughly planned and adjusted to meet the changing needs of each care recipient. Groups are kept small enabling one to one interaction in a calm atmosphere. Personal care such as assistance with toileting is included. Respite is available as a one-off or on a regular basis. The service is available from 8:00am to 4:00pm, Monday to Friday.
