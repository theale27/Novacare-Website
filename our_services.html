Client Information Guide

Novacare Milpara Centre
With activities, outings and entertainment, Milpara provides an opportunity for people to socialise and meet new friends in their community. Transport and meals are available at minimum cost.

Respite Services
We offer a range of services including day and overnight respite to give carers a well earned break. We work closely with carers and families to ensure that we meet the individual needs of the carer and the person being cared for.

Dementia Services
Individually designed services are available for people with dementia. Our dementia care staff are skilled and experienced in caring for people living with dementia. Services include in-home packaged care, centre based care, activities, outings and respite services for carers.

Carers Groups
We understand that caring for someone can be rewarding, challenging and isolating with emotional highs and lows. Carers groups provide a network of support, friendship and information amongst others who may have similar experiences.

Packaged Care
A range of flexible support services are tailored into low and high care packages to help older people to remain at home for as long as possible and to optimise their independence and enjoyment of life.

Domestic Assistance
If you are finding it hard to lift heavy washing or shopping bags or to do household chores, our domestic assistance service may be for you. Designed to meet your needs, this flexible service provides help with day to day tasks including housework, laundry and shopping.

Lawn, Garden & External Home Maintenance
Novacare has a contracted professional lawn, gardening & maintenance services provider, to assist funded and non-funded Novacare clients.

Need help with the exterior of your home?
