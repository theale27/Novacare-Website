Novacare's April Newsletter

Notice of change of name and administration office move

Novacare Incorporated has revised its legal entity structure and trading name and is now known as Novacare Community Services Limited. With our new name comes new Administration Office premises, relocating to Unit 1, 33 Newton, Broadmeadow as at 14 April 2014. Our phone number and postal address remain unchanged.

Public Holiday in April are:
  • Good Friday, 18 April
  • Easter Monday, 21 April
  • Anzac Day, Friday 25 April

Novacare Milpara has loads of great outings and activities planned for April, including:
  • Fort Scratchley Guide Tour and Canon Firing
  • Trip out to Budgewoi
  • Grossman House tour and lunch
  • Concert at Milpara with Adam Price
  • Trip out to Oakvale Farm
  • Performance at Milpara by Ken Cocking
  • Trip to Inner Light House at Nelson Bay
